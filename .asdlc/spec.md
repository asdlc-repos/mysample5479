# Overview

A web app where people create and manage their to-do items, organized by category and due date.

# Who uses it

- Individual users — track their personal tasks and stay organized.

# What they can do

- Someone creates a new task with a title, optional category, and optional due date.
- They mark tasks as complete when finished.
- They edit task details or delete tasks they no longer need.
- They see all their tasks in one place.
- They filter their view to show only tasks in a specific category or due within a certain timeframe.
